About Our Company

Ashwin Gajan T/A Just Parts was founded in 2023, the parent company being Just Parts cc, which is now no longer trading. Our company has quickly established itself as a trusted source for essential automotive solutions. Our product range includes top-selling items such as the Cummins Engine Parts, International (Navistar) Truck Parts, which are engineered to enhance both performance and durability.

We understand that the reliability of engine components, suspension components etc. is critical to the overall functionality of trucks. Therefore, we prioritize our supply of quality products. Our commitment is to ensure that each component meets industry standards and customer expectations and satisfaction. In fact, we maintain a high quality assurance rate, underscoring our dedication to excellence.

As industry experts, we continuously monitor advancements in automotive technology and integrate these innovations into our product range. "Our goal is to support our customers with parts that not only improve performance but also contribute to the longevity of their vehicles". We are proud to serve a diverse clientele, from individual truck fleet owners to large corporate companies in the same line of business as ours. We remain committed to delivering exceptional service and expertise in every interaction.
